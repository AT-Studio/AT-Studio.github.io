Projects

CrazyPage

CrazyPage is a web application where players must catch circles that are travelling around the screen at varying speeds. The higher the speed of the circle the more points a player gets for catching the circle. The application offers a single-player mode and a 2-player mode.

RateIt

RateIt is a social media app that I created as the Capstone Project for my Android Developer Nanodegree. The app allows users to create posts (picture + caption), as well as rate the posts of other users. Similar to the Tinder app, the posts are randomized and you move from one post to another by swiping. In addition to rating posts of other users, users may also comment on posts and add them to their favorites.
